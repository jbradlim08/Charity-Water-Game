Game Rules
Move with Arrow Keys or W A S D.
Collect clean water before it expires.
Every clean water adds 1 growth charge.
At 3 charges, train grows and clean score +1.
Expired clean water becomes dirty water and dirty score +1.
Hit wall or your own train = Game Over.
Timer reaching 0 = Time's Up.
